Noise-generator Synth

Developed by Diego Peralta

Instructions:

  • Make sure you start "AudioContext"
  • The synth has four ranges: low, mid-low, mid-high, and high. Press C3, D3, E3, and F3 respectively on your MIDI keyboard to activate the loops.
  • Pause the synth with the "Stop" button.
  • It is highly encouraged to open the developer tools to check if WebMidi and the AudioContext are enabled.
  • This synth is very loud, make sure to adjust your speakers volume at a soft level
